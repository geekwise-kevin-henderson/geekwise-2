Kevin James Henderson - Web Developer

I was born in Decatur, Alabama on December 29, 1998. Soon after my family moved to Fresno, California; where I lived for the next few years. Until my mother got a job in Pheonix, Arizona. My family lived there for 12 more years, during which my siblings Dylan, Emma and Faith were born.

The summer before Jr. High, my dad left to go work in the oil fields of North Dakota. With no one to help support our family, my mom moved us to Kingsburg, California to be near her family.

I spent two years at Rafer Johnson Junior High in Kingsburg, then I moved on to Kingsburg High School. At KHS, I enrolled in 2 career tech programs. I did 4 years of welding and in my Junior year Bitwise introduced their Geekwise High program, I immediately signed up for it.

I did Geekwise high for my remaining 2 years at KHS. I learned from Bitsise's Climate Amante De La Vida, Benchmark's Ken Kuntz and Bitwise's Matthew Higley. During that two years I interned under Miguel Alarcon at Shift3 industries.
